Weather

Spring: March to May

Mild weather with the famous cherry blossoming.
Spring is considered the best time to visit Japan.

Summer: July to August

Hot and humid. Temperature can exceed 30°C
with average humidity over 70%.

Autumn: September to November

Sunny and comfortable temperatures.
Temperature starts to drop as the humidity eases.

Winter: December to February

Dry and windy with occasional cold rain and snow.
Temperature averages around 5°C

Currency

The legal tender is the Japanese Yen (JPY)
Exchange rates may fluctuate slightly.
Banknotes come in 1000yen, 5000yen and 10000yen.
Coins come in 1yen, 5yen, 10yen, 50yen, 100yen and 500yen (about 500 USD)

Electrical System

Local voltage is 220 to 240 volts AC.
For complimentary adapters, please request the housekeeping.

Mobile Phones

GSM 900 / PCS 1800 / CDMA / WCDMA
Complimentary Wifi inside the hotel
